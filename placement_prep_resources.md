## TCS NQT Exam Structure

- **Part A – Foundation** (75 min total)
  - Numerical Ability: 20 Qs / 25 min
  - Logical Reasoning: 20 Qs / 25 min
  - Verbal Ability: 25 Qs / 25 min
- **Part B – Advanced** (115 min total)
  - Advanced Quant & Reasoning: 14 Qs / 25 min
  - Advanced Coding: 3 Qs / 90 min
- No negative marking
- Cannot go back to previous questions
- Single exam decides: Ninja (₹3.36 LPA) / Digital (₹7 LPA) / Prime (₹9–12 LPA)

## Key Topics to Cover

### Quantitative Aptitude
- Percentages, Profit & Loss
- Ratios & Proportions
- Time & Work, Time & Distance
- Permutation & Combination, Probability
- Number System, HCF & LCM

### Logical Reasoning
- Seating Arrangements
- Blood Relations
- Syllogisms
- Number Series & Patterns
- Coding-Decoding

### Verbal Ability
- Reading Comprehension
- Grammar & Sentence Correction
- Vocabulary

## Strategy
- TCS NQT prep covers ~80% of what's needed for Infosys, Wipro, and Cognizant — do them in parallel
- Use 2-platform stack: one for aptitude (IndiaBix/PrepInsta) + one for coding (LeetCode/HackerRank)
- Each NQT question gets ~90 seconds — speed matters as much as accuracy
- Practice with timed mock tests from Week 1 itself
